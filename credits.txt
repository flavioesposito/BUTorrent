This file sharing client, BUtorrent 1.0, builds on the classic implementation of BitTorrent. 
BUTorrent 1.0 differs from BitTorrent mainly in the seed scheduling algorithm applied at the seed. 

Details of the algorithm and of the protocol were presented at:
The 8th IEEE International Symposium on Network Computing and Applications, Boston, MA  9 - 11 July 2009.
in a paper entitled:
 "Seed Scheduling for Peer-to-Peer Networks"
by 
Flavio Esposito, 
Ibrahim Matta, 
Pietro Michiardi, 
Nobuyuki Mitsutake,
Damiano Carra


Additional info can be found at:
http://csr.bu.edu/butorrent/
